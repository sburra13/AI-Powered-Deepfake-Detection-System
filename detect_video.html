How video analysis works

1

Your video is uploaded to the FastAPI backend and frames are extracted at regular intervals.

2

Each sampled frame is processed by the Haar cascade face detector to isolate the face region.

3

Each face crop is classified individually by the Xception model, producing a per-frame confidence score.

4

Scores are aggregated across all frames into a final verdict. The video is then deleted from the server.

Tip: For accurate results, ensure the video contains a clearly visible face and is at least 3 seconds long. Heavily compressed or low-resolution video may reduce accuracy.

Privacy note: Uploaded videos are deleted immediately after analysis is complete.
