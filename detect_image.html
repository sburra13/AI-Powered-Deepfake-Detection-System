How it works

1

Your image is received by the FastAPI backend and temporarily stored.

2

A Haar cascade face detector isolates the largest face region in the photo.

3

The face crop is normalised and passed through our fine-tuned Xception model.

4

A softmax confidence score is returned — REAL or FAKE — and the file is deleted.

Privacy note: Uploaded images are never stored permanently. Files are deleted immediately after the prediction is returned.
